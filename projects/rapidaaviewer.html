The rapid accuracy assesment viewer is composed of two simple programs designed to accelerate the process of verifying remote sensing data through visually reviewing many specific locations. The first program is the "jpeg generator" which works with ArcGIS by iterating through a shapefile and clipping out the area immediately around it as a jpeg. The intent is to combine view the polygons over different rasters to for the purpose of supervised classification. For example with a change detection application a location of interest may be clipped out of 2-3 different rasters and viewed side by side in the second program, the "viewer."
